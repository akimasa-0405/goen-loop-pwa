A small journey through Goen.

Goen Loop is an experience that begins with matcha and leads you to discover new connections with Kamakura.

When you return your cup, you receive a ¥5 coin, a small symbol of goen.

In Japan, ¥5 is cherished as a coin that represents meaningful connections and good fortune.

Carry it with you, visit a shrine or temple you feel drawn to, and experience your own connection with Kamakura.
