About GOEN LOOP

Return your cup, receive a ¥5 coin, and begin a small journey through Kamakura’s shrines and temples.

FAQ

Do I have to visit a shrine?

No. GOEN LOOP is an invitation, not a rule. Enjoy Kamakura in your own way.

Can I keep the ¥5 coin?

Yes. Many people offer it at a shrine, but you may also keep it as a small memory of your journey.

How do I add this app to my home screen?

On iPhone: tap the share button in Safari, then choose “Add to Home Screen.”
On Android: open the browser menu, then choose “Add to Home Screen.”
